About me

Hola!

My name´s Tirso Sánchez Otaegui, I’m a creative and enthusiashtic graphic designer with experience in web and graphic design, including logo and corporate design. I have experience in big format digital pringting as well.

I have the ability to work as part of a team and I am a good co-worker. I am a hardworking, friendly, creative and responsible person. I am enthusiastic person who enjoys helping others I am an approachable person as well, and enthusiastic about new challenges. Since I have worked in several fields I am able to adapt easily to new working environments. In Spain spent my time in creative agencies such as Propias Publicidad, Nuria Carballo Publicidad and Eurorot, then I moved to London and worked with London Graphics before setting up De Nada, an online creative agency.I'm graduated as Technician in Advertising Graphics, Superior Grade Formative Course - AULA D School of Design, Vigo (Spain). I've got a Degree in Law, Universidad Complutense (Madrid)...yes! I know it sounds strange! ;-D
